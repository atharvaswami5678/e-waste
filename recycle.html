Recycling E-Waste Categories

Cooling Equipment

Recycling cooling equipment is crucial as it helps in reducing electronic waste in landfills. Components like refrigerants and metals can be extracted and reused.

Computers

Recycling computers involves dismantling and reusing valuable components like processors, RAM, and hard drives, while safely disposing of hazardous materials.

Solar Panels

Solar panel recycling aims to recover valuable materials like silicon, aluminum, and glass, reducing the environmental impact of solar energy production.

LED Bulbs

Recycling LED bulbs allows for the extraction of valuable metals like aluminum and copper while ensuring proper disposal of toxic substances.

Vending Machine

Vending machine recycling involves recovering metals, plastics, and electronic components, helping reduce waste and environmental impact.
